>[!note] Identity Matrix
>An identity [[2.4b Special Matrices|matrix]] $I \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$,
>$$I =
>\begin{bmatrix}
>1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ 
>0 & 1 & \dots & 0 \\
>\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
>0 & 0 & \dots & 1
>\end{bmatrix},$$
>is a square matrix whose diagonal elements are equal to 1.
